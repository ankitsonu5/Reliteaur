The Origin Story: A Kitchen Revolution

There was a Time...

When "gluten-free" meant "guilt-free". Where celiacs apologized for their dietary needs. Where gut-healthy food was seen as bland food.

Then Everything Changed.

When our founder Raguram Raghulal embraced a gluten-free lifestyle, he didn’t settle—he innovated. Like every gluten-free food seeker, he found himself asking the same questions: “Will I have to compromise on taste? Is it right for my palate? Will I miss out on delicious food?”

He turned this concern and empathy into a tenacious drive towards making gluten-free cuisines desirable as any other loved tasty fare.

Armed with decades of baking wizardry and an obsession with perfection, we set out to prove that gluten-free ingredients can indeed help you whip up the tastiest dishes. No love lost: only your tastebuds loving every bite.

oday, we're rewriting the rules of what gluten-free can be. One delectable bite at a time.
